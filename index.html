How it works

Three simple steps. One SMS confirmation.

1

Add your child’s number

Enter your child’s mobile. We text you to confirm you’re the parent or guardian.

2

We mark it under‑16

SafeGen labels that number on the SafeList as belonging to a minor until their 16th birthday month.

3

Apps check & block

Social platforms query the SafeList at signup/login. If it’s a minor’s number, access is blocked.

Why parents trust SafeGen

  • We store only phone numbers and your consent record.
  • No IDs, photos, or biometrics — ever.
  • Platforms get a yes/no via a query‑only API. No data resale.
  • You can remove or update a number at any time.

FAQ

What if my child turns 16?
We automatically remove the under‑16 flag at their birthday month, and notify you beforehand.
Can I remove a number?
Yes, anytime via the SMS link we send or by contacting support. Removal is immediate.
Which apps use SafeGen?
We’re onboarding major platforms via the pilot API. We’ll publish a live list as partners go public.
What if my child changes SIM?
Update the number in your parent portal or re‑add the new number. The old one is automatically removed.
Is this government‑run?
No. SafeGen is a parent‑driven service aligned with Australian online‑safety goals without burdening all adults.
How is the counter calculated?
It shows confirmed parent enrollments, updated every minute with local caching for speed.
