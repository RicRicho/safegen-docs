# 05. Business Model & Unit Economics

## Revenue Streams
1. **Platform Subscription (70% of ARR)**
   - Tiered pricing based on emissions volume processed and module access.
   - Annual contracts with auto-renewal and 8% annual uplift clause.
2. **Professional Services (20% of ARR)**
   - Implementation, data cleansing, and bespoke modeling services billed at $180/hour blended rate.
   - Average initial engagement: $45K.
3. **Marketplace & Offsets (10% of ARR)**
   - 12% transaction fee on verified carbon credit purchases.
   - Revenue share on partner solutions integrated through Carbon Twin API.

## Pricing Tiers
| Plan | Target Customer | Annual Price | Included Modules |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Core | Single-region manufacturers | $85K | Scope 1 & 2 reporting, workflow automation, audit evidence |
| Growth | Multi-site industrials | $140K | Core + Scope 3 modeling, supplier ledger, API access |
| Enterprise | Global supply chains | $240K+ | Growth + marketplace, custom analytics, dedicated CSM |

## Unit Economics (FY24)
- **Gross Margin:** 78%
- **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC):** $83K
- **Customer Lifetime Value (CLTV):** $672K
- **CLTV/CAC Ratio:** 8.1x
- **Payback Period:** 9 months

## Churn & Expansion
- Gross churn: 3%
- Expansion ARR: 32% from module upsells and increased data volumes.
- Top expansion levers: Supplier Impact Ledger (47% attach rate), Assurance Module (38% attach rate).

## Path to Profitability
- Break-even target: Q4 FY26 at $22M ARR.
- Hiring plan: +12 GTM, +8 engineers, +3 customer success over next 12 months.
- Efficiency initiatives: AI-driven onboarding reducing implementation hours by 25%, expanding partner-led deployments.
