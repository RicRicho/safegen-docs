# 06. Go-To-Market Strategy

## Sales Motion
- **Target Accounts:** 600 named industrial and logistics companies; 3-tier prioritization based on emissions volume, regulatory exposure, and data maturity.
- **Sales Team:** 6 enterprise AEs, 3 solutions engineers, 4 BDRs. Average quota attainment 112%.
- **Methodology:** MEDDICC qualification, with sustainability impact assessments delivered during discovery.
- **Pilot Framework:** 90-day scoped engagement culminating in reduction roadmap and ROI analysis; converts to multi-year subscription 78% of the time.

## Marketing Engine
- **Demand Generation:**
  - Quarterly virtual summits with 1,200+ registrants.
  - ABM campaigns targeting operations and sustainability leaders with personalized benchmarks.
  - Content syndication focused on CSRD readiness and supplier decarbonization.
- **Brand & Thought Leadership:**
  - Carbon Intensity Index published bi-annually, referenced by GreenBiz and SupplyChainDive.
  - Participation in WEF and Climate Week panels.
- **Product Marketing:** ROI calculators, industry-specific solution briefs, analyst relations (Gartner Cool Vendor 2024 nominee).

## Partnerships
- **Technology:** SAP, Microsoft, Snowflake data sharing, IoT sensor providers.
- **Consulting & Audit:** KPMG climate practice, LRQA for verification services.
- **Logistics Programs:** Carrier alliances offering low-carbon shipping options with embedded CarbonKilos data.

## Customer Success & Expansion
- Dedicated Customer Success Manager for every account >$120K ARR.
- Quarterly Business Reviews with emissions progress scorecards and roadmap alignment.
- Community portal facilitating supplier onboarding and sharing of reduction best practices.
