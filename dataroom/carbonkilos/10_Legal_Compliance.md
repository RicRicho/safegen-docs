# 10. Legal, Compliance & Risk

- Board of Directors: Maya Chen (CEO), Luca Weber (CTO), Gabriel Thompson (NorthBridge Ventures), Sofia Nilsson (Independent).

## Intellectual Property

## Regulatory Compliance
- Adheres to EU CSRD, UK SECR, and proposed US SEC climate disclosure frameworks.
- Supports GHG Protocol Corporate Standard (Scopes 1-3) and Product Standard; aligned with ISO 14064-1 and ISO 14067.
- Marketplace projects verified by Gold Standard, Verra, and Puro.earth methodologies.
- Conducts annual third-party audits for PCAF accreditation.

## Contracts & Liabilities
- Standard MSA includes data processing addendum, liability cap at 2x annual fees, and SLA with service credits.
- Cyber liability insurance: $5M coverage.
- Professional indemnity insurance: $3M coverage for advisory components.
- No outstanding litigation; resolved a minor supplier dispute in March 2024 with no financial impact.

## Risk Management
- Quarterly enterprise risk assessment covering regulatory, operational, cyber, and market risks.
- Business continuity plan tested semi-annually with tabletop exercises.
- Vendor risk management program with pre-qualification scoring and annual reviews.

## Environmental & Social Governance (ESG)
- Internal carbon neutrality maintained through renewable energy contracts and offsets.
- Diversity metrics published annually; 52% of employees identify as women, 38% from underrepresented groups.
- Ethical AI guidelines enforce human review for material recommendations and customer transparency.
