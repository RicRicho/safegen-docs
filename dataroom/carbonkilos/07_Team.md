## Culture & Values
1. **Integrity of Impact:** Decisions grounded in verifiable emissions reductions.
2. **Operational Transparency:** Open dashboards for KPIs, sprint velocity, and customer health.
3. **Customer Co-Creation:** Product squads embed with customers for quarterly innovation sprints.
4. **Inclusive Growth:** DEI hiring goals with 55% of leadership identifying as women or underrepresented minorities.
