# 01. Executive Summary

## Mission & Vision
CarbonKilos enables mid-market manufacturers to quantify, reduce, and report carbon intensity across logistics and production. Our platform simplifies greenhouse gas (GHG) accounting with granular SKU-level insights, automations, and verified offsets, helping customers hit science-based targets while unlocking revenue from low-carbon product lines.

## Investment Ask
- **Round:** $12M Series A (equity)
- **Use of Proceeds:**
  - 45% product & engineering (scope 3 modeling, audit automation)
  - 30% go-to-market (enterprise sales, partner ecosystem)
  - 15% verified carbon project procurement
  - 10% working capital & contingency
- **Runway:** 24 months at current burn.

## 2024 Highlights
- Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR) of **$4.1M**, up 210% year-over-year.
- Net Revenue Retention (NRR) of **129%**, driven by module expansions and premium data services.
- Landed strategic partnerships with **Maersk EcoDelivery** and **Siemens Mobility** for integrated emissions tracking.
- Released AI-driven "Carbon Twin" optimizer, reducing customer emissions intensity by 18% on average within six months.

## Strategic Rationale
Investment accelerates CarbonKilos' leadership in verifiable carbon intelligence for the industrial supply chain, a category projected to exceed $12B globally by 2028. The company combines proprietary life-cycle analysis (LCA) models with certified audit workflows, creating high switching costs and defensible data moats.
