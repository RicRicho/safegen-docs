# 03. Market Landscape

## Problem Statement
Industrial manufacturers and logistics firms face mounting pressure to disclose and reduce Scope 1-3 emissions. Regulations (EU CSRD, SEC climate disclosure), customer procurement requirements, and lender ESG covenants require auditable data and demonstrable reductions. Current processes rely on manual spreadsheets, static averages, and fragmented consultants, creating compliance risk and missed savings.

## Target Customers
| Segment | Description | Pain Points | Buying Trigger |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Mid-market manufacturers ($200M-$2B revenue) | Specialty metals, automotive components, industrial machinery | Lack granular supplier emissions data, complex multi-site operations | CSRD reporting deadlines, customer RFP requirements |
| Global logistics providers | Freight forwarders, 3PLs, maritime carriers | Difficulty measuring emissions intensity per lane, pressure for green offerings | Shipper demand for low-carbon SLAs |
| Consumer durables brands | Appliances, electronics | Supplier compliance tracking, brand reputation risk | Retailer scorecards, internal net-zero commitments |
